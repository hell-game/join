Sound & Feedback That Feel Good

Quick audio pops and subtle visuals make every tap rewarding.

Published 2025-08-22

Crisp sound design and light visual touches turn routine actions into satisfying moments of play.

Micro sounds

Use tight, punchy cues to avoid audio delay and keep interactions snappy.

Visual flash

Brief glows, pulses, or gentle shakes celebrate success without distraction.

Volume respect

Always include a mute option and honor system volume for player comfort.
